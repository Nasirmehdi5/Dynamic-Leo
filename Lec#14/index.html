DeepSeek

DeepSeek is a Chinese artificial intelligence research company that focuses on developing large language models (LLMs) and other AI technologies. They are known for creating DeepSeek-V2, a powerful open-source LLM that competes with models like GPT-4 and Llama 3. DeepSeek specializes in both text-based and code-generation AI, and they have also developed a code-focused model called DeepSeek-Coder, which is trained to assist with programming tasks.
