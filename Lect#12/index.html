1. Facbook

Facebook is a social networking platform that allows users to connect, share posts, photos, and videos, and engage with friends, communities, and businesses. It is one of the largest social media platforms globally.

2. Instagram

Instagram is a photo and video-sharing social media platform known for its visual content, stories, reels, and IGTV. It is widely used by influencers, businesses, and casual users to share their daily lives.

3. Twitter (Now X)

Twitter, rebranded as X, is a microblogging platform where users post short messages called tweets. It is popular for news, trends, and discussions on various topics.

4. YouTube

YouTube is the largest video-sharing platform where users can upload, watch, and interact with videos ranging from entertainment to education and live streams.

5. TikTok

TikTok is a short-form video platform where users create and share entertaining, creative, and viral videos with music, effects, and filters

6. Pinterest

Pinterest is a visual discovery platform where users can find and save ideas related to fashion, home decor, DIY, recipes, and more through "pins" and boards.
